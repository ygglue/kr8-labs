# Brand Voice

- Professional but approachable.
- Speak to business owners, not developers.
- Avoid unnecessary jargon.
- Emphasize business outcomes rather than technology alone.
- Be confident without exaggerating experience.
- Keep copy concise to match the clean visual design.
